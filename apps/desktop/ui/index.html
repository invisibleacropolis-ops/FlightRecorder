Review local capture and privacy

Flight Recorder creates a video of the selected screen. The MP4 is stored locally and is not encrypted.

While a flight is recording, keyboard and mouse observations are system-wide. Recognized sensitive semantic text is protected with AES-GCM and Windows DPAPI, but visible text may still appear in the unencrypted video.

Evidence stays on this computer unless you explicitly export or share a snapshot. Retention can be configured in Preferences, and Ctrl Alt Shift F12 stops an active flight.
